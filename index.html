Ahmad Shah Baba


Ahmad Shah Baba ,also know as Ahamd shah Durrani , founded the Durrani empire in 1774,laying the foundation for modern Afghanistan . Revered as the "Father of the Nation" he united the Afghan tribes , expanded his empire across South and Central Asia , and established a legacy of Afghan sovereignty and resilience . his leadership and miitary prowsess earned him a lasting place in the history of the region.

Here's a time line of Ahmad Shah Durrani(Baba) life:


  • 1722 - Born in Herat
  • 1747 - He had established his self as a formidable leader ,uniting various Afghan tribes under his rule .this year marked the founding of the Durrani empire ,with Ahmad Shah proclamed as its first ruler
  • 1757 - He led a successful expedition to conquer Delhi, established control over significant parts of present-day India. His strategic prowess and diplomatic skills allowed him to forge alliances and extend his influence across South and Central Asia.
  • 1777 - After a region marked by significant achievments and challenges , Ahmad Shah Baba passed away, leaving behind a legacy as the "Father of the Nation" in Afghanistan . His leadership and vision for united Afghan state continue to inpire reverence and admiration ,shaping the course of Afghan history for generations to come.
